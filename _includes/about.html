About Spotlight Infosec

We work with our customers to achieve their Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) and cyber security goals.

Our experts understand the complex landscape of their fields and ensure that their custom solutions become actionable results for our customers.

Micah "Webbreacher" Hoffman, principal investigator and owner of Spotlight Infosec LLC, has been in the cyber security and OSINT fields for over 15 years. Since 2014, Micah has taught courses for the SANS Institute and in 2018, he completed and launched SANS's first OSINT course, SEC487. An avid OSINT researcher and teacher, Micah maintains a personal web site at WebBreacher.com that has links to blog posts and videos of his webcasts and conference talks.
